2. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Sport Counter.

2.1 Data We Collect

We use Firebase Authentication (by Google LLC) to allow secure sign-in with your Google account. When you sign in, Firebase provides us with your email address and a unique user ID (UID). We store this information in our secure database to link your personal account data and ensure that only you can access it.

Additionally, we store your usage data (such as selected resorts and related expenses) linked to your UID. We do not collect or process any other personal information.

2.2 Purpose and Legal Basis

We process your personal data solely to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Provide personalized access to your data
  • Calculate and display your resort statistics

The legal basis under Article 6(1)(b) GDPR is the performance of a contract, meaning this data is necessary for Sport Counter to function.

2.3 Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secure database hosted within the European Union by a GDPR-compliant hosting provider. All information is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections, and access to the database is restricted to authorised system components only.

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures (such as access control, regular security updates, etc.) to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, or loss. No personal data are transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA) in connection with hosting.

2.4 Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase may process authentication data (email address and UID) on servers located outside the EU, under the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework, ensuring an adequate level of data protection. Google does not use Firebase Authentication data for advertising or profiling purposes.

2.5 User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Receive a copy of your data

You can exercise these rights at any time by contacting us at barile.cec@gmail.com. Requests will be processed within 30 days.

2.6 Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as your account exists. If you request deletion of your account, all related personal and usage data are permanently removed from our systems.

2.7 Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
